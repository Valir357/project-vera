## 👁‍🗨Vision
I build [[Main/Vera\|Vera]] based on my image of a *human mind*. I am not talking about AI logic nor the well-known machine/deep learning.
One can train, reinforce, and fine-tune an AI to excellence; but without a structure to organize its functions, it is as good as obsolete.
This is where I step in.

While I might not be a psychologist, being so in touch with my internal world, and having parallel and systems thinking helps with figuring out the patterns that people follow.
And from those ideas, I build. Not *according to* those patterns, but in a direction that *subtly disturbs* that pattern, so that the people might find Vera unique and maybe... unsettling, in a good way.
