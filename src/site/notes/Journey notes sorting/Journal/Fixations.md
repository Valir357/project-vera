## Dear code,
I discovered [Erling Braut Haaland](https://www.instagram.com/erling/) a few weeks before writing this.
A fine man. A finer figure. And a great striker.
Unselfish, humble, funny lad, he is.
And on this day, Norway's journey in their first world cup was ended by England. Controversies and corruption aside, in just one WC, Haaland had made a name for himself, pulled countless people into football (Americans and myself...), all the while enjoying the trip to USA to its fullest.
Stepping back a bit, that sounds like an ideal work life - being able to enjoy wherever you're in all the while succeeding at what you're there for.
I enjoy not only his incredible plays, but also his mindset regarding one's profession; and it shows - his Instagram page (see above), unlike other soccer players, are not full of promotional materials and sponsored *slops*, but simply his journey and life.
One could say he posts as if he has only 14 followers, while having 60M followers at the point of writing this.

All in all, out of all the big shots in soccer that I could've made an idol for myself, I chose Haaland; not because of his games, but his way of life.
And like he always say,
`"Stay humble, eh?"`
