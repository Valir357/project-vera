## Vision

I build [[Main/Vera\|Vera]] upon my image of a "human mind". I am not talking about the already-existing learning function of AIs, but the thinking process, reactions, and more importantly, memory.
For non-coders, one might think that for memories, just throw in whatever facts and interactions and let the AI store them. But that is not it.
The human mind is complex. One might remember events of their childhood and forget things happening just 5 minutes ago. But such happenings are not always accidental, and can be explained properly.
Taking inspiration from DungeonAI's memory system, 

While I might not be a psychologist, being so in touch with my internal world, mindscape, and having parallel thinking helps with figuring out the patterns that most people follow.
And from those ideas, I build. Not according to those patterns, but in a direction that subtly disturbs that pattern, so that the people might find Vera unique and perhaps... disturbing, in a good way.
